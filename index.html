Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your score. You win the game by making your total score to random number, you lose the game is your total score goes about the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
